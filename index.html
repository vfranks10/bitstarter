FAQ

What is the Center for Art and Technology?
A place of learning. We will provide an enriching environment for young adults to explore and grow. In addition there will be an adult education program to help people gain skills for in- demand careers. We are working with employers to make sure that these skills will be put to use upon graduation.
Why a Center for Art and Technology?
This center is going to be a place for school children to explore as well as an adult education program.
